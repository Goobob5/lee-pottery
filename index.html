The maker

Richard
Lee

I'm Richard Lee — originally from New Zealand, making in Sydney. My work explores organic themes through form, illustration, and surface design. All pieces are original and one of a kind.

The practice is constantly evolving: explore an idea, iterate, create, then move on. Nothing gets repeated indefinitely.

The illustrations are painted freehand before firing — swans, crabs, crocodiles, snakes. Animals that feel both ancient and immediate.
